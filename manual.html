User Manual (V6.2)

Welcome to Prompt Station. This version is now fully free, including unlimited tags and Local AI support.

1. Quick Start

Step 1: Install the extension and pin it to your browser toolbar.

Step 2: Open ChatGPT, Gemini, or Copilot.

Step 3: Click the extension icon and select your tags (e.g., Translate + Traditional Chinese).

Step 4: Ensure the chat input is EMPTY, then press Enter. The prompt will be injected automatically.

âš ï¸ Note: If the input box contains any text, the extension will disable injection to allow you to send your message normally (preventing overwrite).

2. Local AI Setup (Ollama)

V6.2 supports local model injection.

  • Default Support: Works with http://localhost:3000 (Open WebUI) and standard ports.
  • Custom Domain: Scroll to the bottom of the extension popup, enter your IP (e.g., 192.168.1.50), and click Add.

3. Backup & Restore

Regularly backup your library to prevent data loss.

Click [Export JSON] inside the extension to download a backup. Use [Import JSON] to restore your settings on a new device.

FAQ

Q: Why does nothing happen when I press Enter?

A: Please check if the chat box is empty. If there is any text (even a space), the extension will safeguard your input and NOT inject prompts.

Q: Where is the "Upgrade to PRO" button?

A: We have made all PRO features free! You don't need a license key anymore. Just install and enjoy unlimited access.

Q: Where is my data stored?

A: Your data is stored 100% locally in your browser (Local Storage). We have no cloud database and do not track your usage.
