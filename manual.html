User Manual (V6.4)

Welcome to Prompt Station V6.4, featuring the built-in "Quick Capture Inbox" for seamless prompt collection.

1. Quick Start (Injecting Prompts)

Step 1: Click the extension icon and select your tags.

Step 2: Ensure the chat input is EMPTY, then press Enter.

Supported: ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, DeepSeek, and Local AI (Ollama).

2. Quick Capture (Inbox)

Save prompts without breaking your flow!

  • Capture: Select text on any webpage â” Right-click â” Select Save to Prompt Station.
  • Review: Open the extension popup. You'll see a new âš¡ Inbox section at the top.
  • Save: Click [Edit] next to an item to load it into the editor, then save it as a permanent tag.

3. Local AI Setup

Supports Localhost and Custom Domains.

  • Default: Works with http://localhost:3000 and standard ports.
  • Custom: Enter your IP in the settings area and click Add to support private servers.

FAQ

Q: Where is Inbox data stored?

A: 100% locally in your browser (Chrome Storage Local). No cloud sync.

Q: Why don't I see the right-click menu?

A: Ensure you have selected text. The menu item is hidden when no text is selected.
