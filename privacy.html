Privacy Policy for Prompt Station

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Prompt Station ("the Extension") is designed with privacy as its core principle. We adhere to a strict "Local-First" policy.

1. Data Collection & Local Storage

  • Local Storage: All your custom prompts, rules, Inbox items, and user settings are stored exclusively on your device using Chrome's storage.local API.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not transmit, sync, or backup your data to any external servers. If you uninstall the extension, your data is removed (unless you have manually exported a JSON backup).
  • Chat History: The "Export Chat" feature processes data temporarily in your browser memory to generate the markdown file. We do not store or upload your conversation history.

2. Permissions & Usage

We request the minimum permissions necessary for functionality:

  • Context Menus (contextMenus): This permission is used solely to add the "Save to Prompt Station" option to your browser's right-click menu. We only access the text you specifically select and choose to save. We do not monitor other browsing activities.
  • Host Permissions: Used to inject functionality into AI platforms (ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot) and to enable the "Custom Domain" feature for Local AI users. We do not track your browsing history on other websites.

3. Third-Party Services

  • No Tracking: The Extension does not collect usage analytics or user behavior data.
  • Donations: We may use PayPal/Stripe for optional donations. We do not handle or store any financial data.
