Privacy Policy for Prompt Station

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

1. Data Collection & Local Storage

Prompt Station ("the Extension") adheres to a strict "Local-First" policy.

  • Local Storage: All your custom prompts, rules, tag groups, and settings are stored exclusively on your device using Chrome's storage.local API.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not transmit, sync, or backup your data to any external servers. If you uninstall the Extension, your local data will be removed.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or share your personal information, browsing history, or chat content.

2. Payment & Licenses

  • Free & Open: The Extension is fully free to use. We do not collect payment information, credit card details, or verify license keys.
  • Donations: If you choose to donate via PayPal, the transaction is handled entirely by PayPal. We do not receive or store your financial details.

3. Third-Party & Local AI Services

The Extension interacts with third-party platforms solely to function as a text-insertion tool.

  • Cloud Platforms (ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot): The Extension injects text into the input field at your command. It does not read, save, or upload your conversation history.
  • Local AI (Ollama, WebUI): The Extension supports interaction with local servers (e.g., localhost, 127.0.0.1) or custom domains defined by you. Data sent to these endpoints remains within your local network or your specified private server. We do not have access to these interactions.

4. Permissions

  • Host Permissions: The Extension requests broad host permissions (*://*/*) solely to enable the "Custom Domain" feature, allowing you to use the tool on private AI servers. We do not track your browsing activity on other websites.
