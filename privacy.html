Privacy Policy for Prompt Station

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

1. Data Collection & Local Storage

Prompt Station ("the Extension") adheres to a strict "Local-First" policy.

  • Local Storage: All your custom prompts, rules, Inbox items, and settings are stored exclusively on your device using Chrome's storage.local API.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not transmit, sync, or backup your data to any external servers.

2. Permissions & Usage

We request the minimum permissions necessary for functionality:

  • Context Menus (contextMenus): This permission is used solely to add the "Save to Prompt Station" option to your right-click menu. We only access the text you specifically select and choose to save. We do not monitor other browsing activities.
  • Host Permissions: Used to enable the "Custom Domain" feature (for Local AI) and to inject scripts into AI chat platforms (ChatGPT, Gemini, etc.) to perform text insertion.

3. Third-Party Services

  • No Tracking: The Extension does not collect usage analytics or user behavior data.
  • Payments: We use PayPal for optional donations. We do not handle any financial data.
